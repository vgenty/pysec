Activity Analysis
	 inventory turnover days = 365/(cost of goods sold/average inventory)
	 accounts receivable turnover days = 365/(credit sales/average accounts receivable)
	 accounts payable turnover days = 365/(purchases / average accounts payable)
	 cash conversion cycle (CCC) = A/R days + inventory days - A/P days === A/R = accounts receivable, A/P == accounts payable
Liquidity Analysis
	 current ratio = current asses / current liabilities
	 quick ratio = (cash + marketable securities + accounts receivable) / ( current liabilities )
	 cash ratio = (cash + marketable securities) / current liabilities
Long term debt and Solvency Analysis
     	  debt to capital ratio = local debt / ( total debt + total equity )
	  debt to equity ratio  = local debt / (total equity)
	  times interest earned = EBIT / interest
Proftability analysis
	  gross margin = gross profit / sales
	  operating margin = operating income / sales
	  profit margin = net income / sales
Return analysis
          return on assets = net income / total assets
	  return on total capital = net income / ( total debt + total equity)
	  reuturn on equity = net income / total equity
Market analysis
          price to earnings ratio = stock price / earnings per share
	  earnings yield = earnings per share / market price per share
	  dividend yeidl = dividends per share / market price per share
	  dividence payout equity = dividends per share / earnings per share
	  price to book ratio = market price per share / book price per share
EVA (economic value added)
    	  net operating profi after taxes - capital * cost of capital
